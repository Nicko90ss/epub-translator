A student lived in a quiet town. His name was Naoto, and he lived the same life every day. All I had to do was wake up in the morning, go to school, take classes, come home, and study again. He had no particular dreams and was only worried about the future. One rainy day, Naoto stopped by an old library. There were a lot of old books that no one read. Inside, he found a small notebook. On the cover was simply written ``Record of Memories.'' Naoto became interested and opened the notebook. Inside were short diaries written by strangers. Joys, regrets, failures, and small happinesses were carefully recorded. While reading it, Naoto noticed something. Life is not just made up of special events, but rather an accumulation of small everyday moments. From that day on, his world began to change little by little. The beauty of the morning light, a conversation with a friend, the sound of the wind. Things that I didn't pay attention to before now have meaning. For the first time, he wanted to record his life. Naoto carried a notebook with him every day and began writing down what he felt. It was awkward at first, but gradually the words began to flow from my heart. he understood. I can't see the future yet, but this moment is definitely mine. And he was no longer ruled by anxiety. Instead, a quiet hope was growing in my heart.
